How It Works

Simple. Transparent. Life-Saving.

From registration to transplant — ODMS coordinates every step.

01

Register Your Intent

Donors and recipients register through our secure public portal. All data goes directly into Oracle 21c.

02

Medical Review

Partner hospitals and certified doctors review eligibility, blood compatibility, and urgency levels.

03

Coordinated Transplant

Once approved, the donation is tracked end-to-end. Audit logs capture every status change automatically.
